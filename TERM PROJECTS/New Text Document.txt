To better understanding the basics of ZigBee, our group decided to build an actual ZigBee network. One popular module that deploys the ZigBee protocol is the XBee module. There are various XBee versions availble on the market, and our group selected XBee S2C Version E Module as the main testing device. This is an RF module that is designed to communicate or exchange data wirelessly by sitting on top of IEEE 802.15.4 layers.
